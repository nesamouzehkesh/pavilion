About Us

We are a family of designers and craftsmen working together to revive the most iconic product of the Iranian art and culture, the Persian carpet. You can mix the authenticity of the handmade Persian carpets with any design of your own. Your carpet will be woven by skilful hands of our craftsmen, with knots of silk and wool on strings of love and passion. The product is a masterpiece with quality of ancient persian carpets and the unique design of your own taste.

Our Story

We began our work with a passion in art representing the roots of our rich culture and history of ancient Iran. We are a group of artists and creators working as a family who enjoy their work and pledge their efforts towards customers’ satisfaction and producing creative eye-catching designs that stand out from its competitors in the industry. Our unique online customization facility is developed to follow customers’ insights as closely as possible and bring their ideas into life in the most flawless and perfect way.
