About Us

We are a family of designers and craftsmen working together to revive the most iconic product of the Iranian art and culture, the Persian carpet. You can mix the authenticity of the handmade Persian carpets with any design of your own. Your carpet will be woven by skilful hands of our craftsmen, with knots of silk and wool on strings of love and passion. The product is a masterpiece with quality of ancient persian carpets and the unique design of your own taste.

Our Story
